1A Whole cell fixation (for cell lines)
<Note> Cool centrifuge to 4 C and pellet cells at 300 rcf for 51 min throughout
Transfer 5 million cells in growth medium to a 15 ml tube and pellet
Wash once: with 10 ml of ice-cold 1x PBS (resuspending carefully) and pellet
Fix cells in 5 ml ice-cold methanol at -20 °C for 10 min
Wash 2x with. 5 ml ice-cold PBS-BSA-SUPERase
Resuspend cells in 200 ul ofi ice-cold PBS-BSA-SUPERase
Filter through cell strainer (40 um or 70 um depending on cell size)
Use 5 ul of the sample for cell counting (in duplicates), dilute to 5,000 cells/ul with ice-cold PBS-BSA-SUPERase
Proceed mmediately to step 2 (reverse transcription)
1B Nuclei isolation (for cell lines)
Transfer 5 million cells in growth medium to a 15 ml tube and pellet (300 rcf, 5 min, room temperature)
Wash once with 10 ml ofi ice-cold 1x PBS and pellet (300 rcf, 5 min, 4 °C)
Resuspend in 500 uli ice-cold Nuclei Preparation Buffer
Incubate on ice for 5 min to lyse plasma membrane
Add 5 ml ofi ice-cold Nuclei Wash Buffer and pellet (500 rcf, 5 min, 4 °C)
Resuspend nuclei in 200 ul of ice-cold PBS-BSA-SUPERase
Filter through cell strainer (40 um or 70 um depending on cell size)
Use 5 ul of the sample for nuclei counting (in duplicates), dilute to 5,000 nuclei/ul with ice-cold PBS-BSA-SUPERase
Proceed immediately to step 2 (reverse transcription)

with Ampligase Reaction buffer, as described in steps 4A and 4B of this protocol. In these cases we recommend to
perform the thermoligation in SCATAC Barcoding Reagent supplemented with NAD+ and magnesium for compatibil-
ity with the Ampligase enzyme.
<Note> This procedure has been tested for SCATAC v1.0 reagents only.

Add Barcoding Reagent to a total volume of 9.2 ul
